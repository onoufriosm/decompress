# Marketing Directory

This directory contains all marketing operations for Decompress - a tool that helps users keep up with overwhelming podcast and video content through digestible summaries and AI-powered exploration.

## Purpose

This is the central hub for all marketing activities including:
- Content creation and copywriting
- Social media campaigns
- Landing page copy and messaging
- Email marketing materials
- Brand guidelines and assets
- Marketing analytics and tracking
- Growth experiments and A/B tests

## Product Overview (for marketing context)

**Decompress** reduces information overload from podcasts and video content while retaining valuable insights.

### Key Value Propositions
- Subscribe to YouTube channels and podcasts, get automatic summaries
- Daily/weekly digests of new content
- AI-powered chat to explore video transcripts
- Research across your entire video library
- Smart deduplication to skip redundant content (future)

### Target Audience
- Knowledge workers drowning in podcast/video content
- Researchers who need to extract insights from video
- Professionals who want to stay informed without spending hours watching

## Directory Structure

Organize marketing materials in subdirectories as needed:
- `copy/` - Website copy, landing pages, product descriptions
- `social/` - Social media content and campaigns
- `email/` - Email templates and sequences
- `assets/` - Marketing images, graphics, brand materials
- `analytics/` - Marketing metrics and reports
- `experiments/` - A/B tests and growth experiments
