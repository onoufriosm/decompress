# Decompress - Current Features

This document outlines the features currently implemented in Decompress.

## Core Value Proposition

**Turn 2-hour podcasts into 5-minute reads.** Decompress helps busy tech professionals stay informed without drowning in content.

---

## Implemented Features

### 1. AI-Powered Video Summaries
- Automatic AI-generated summaries for YouTube videos and podcasts
- Summaries extract key insights, main arguments, and actionable takeaways
- Skips intros, ads, and tangents to deliver pure signal
- Summaries displayed on video detail pages and in digest views

### 2. Channel Subscriptions
- Browse and discover curated tech podcast channels (a16z, 20VC, All-In, Lenny's Podcast, etc.)
- Subscribe to favorite channels using the favorite button
- Filter channels by category
- Search channels by name, handle, or description
- Request new channels to be added to the platform

### 3. Daily & Weekly Email Digests
- **Daily Digest**: Receive a summary of yesterday's videos every morning
- **Weekly Digest**: Receive a summary of the week's videos every Sunday
- Personalized to your favorited channels
- Toggle digest preferences in profile settings
- Email includes video thumbnails, summaries, and direct links

### 4. Personalized Home Feed ("Your Digest")
- Daily and weekly views of new content from subscribed channels
- Videos grouped by channel for easy scanning
- Toggle summaries on/off for quick browsing
- Badge indicators showing summary availability
- Shows video count per time period

### 5. Video Library & Search
- Full archive access to all indexed videos
- Search videos by title or description
- Filter by category
- View count, duration, and publish date displayed
- Shows hosts and guests appearing in each video

### 6. Video Detail Pages
- Full AI summary with markdown formatting
- Video metadata (duration, views, publish date)
- Direct link to watch on YouTube
- People section showing hosts and guests
- Expandable video description

### 7. AI Chat with Transcripts
- Chat with individual video transcripts to explore topics deeper
- Select multiple videos to chat across their content
- Threaded conversations that persist between sessions
- 200 queries per month limit
- Requires videos to have transcripts

### 8. People Directory
- Browse hosts and guests across all indexed content
- Filter by hosts only or guests only
- Search by name or bio
- View appearance counts (how many channels they host, how many videos they appear in)
- Person detail pages with full bio and social links

### 9. Channel Detail Pages
- Channel info with subscriber count and video count
- List of all videos from the channel
- Favorite/unfavorite channel directly

### 10. User Authentication & Accounts
- Sign up / Sign in with email (magic link or OAuth)
- Profile settings page
- Update display name
- Manage email digest preferences

### 11. Subscription & Billing
- 7-day free trial (no credit card required)
- Pro subscription at $8/month
- Stripe integration for payments
- Manage subscription through Stripe billing portal
- Trial expiration handling with upgrade prompts

### 12. Live Digest Preview (Landing Page)
- Public preview of today's digest on the landing page
- Shows 3 recent videos with AI summaries
- No sign-in required to browse
