# Decompress

A tool to help you keep up with the overwhelming amount of podcast and video content by creating digestible summaries and enabling intelligent exploration.

## Goal

Reduce information overload from podcasts and video content while retaining the valuable insights.

## Core Features

### Channel Subscriptions & Summaries
- Subscribe to YouTube channels and podcasts
- Automatically generate summaries of new episodes
- Get daily/weekly digests of new content

### AI-Powered Chat
- Chat with individual video transcripts to explore topics deeper
- Chat across multiple videos to find connections and patterns
- Research mode for contextual exploration similar to ChatGPT/Gemini but grounded in your subscribed content

### Smart Deduplication (Future)
- Detect overlapping content across videos (e.g., same guest on multiple podcasts)
- Highlight only new information and insights
- Skip redundant content entirely

## Use Cases

1. **Stay informed without drowning** - Get the key points from hours of content in minutes
2. **Deep dive when needed** - Use AI chat to explore specific topics from the transcripts
3. **Research assistant** - Query across your video library for specific information
4. **Avoid repetition** - Smart detection of rehashed content across different appearances
